How to use AllThingsFitness

Here's a quick guide to get the most out of the app.

1

Set up your profile

Head to Update Profile and fill in your age, weight, height, fitness level, and goal. This lets the app personalise everything for you.

2

Generate a workout plan

Go to Workouts and hit Generate Plan. The app will build a personalised multi-day plan based on your profile. Save it to keep it on your dashboard.

3

Check your dashboard

Your Dashboard shows today's workout from your saved plan and your daily calorie and macro targets — all calculated from your profile.

4

Chat with your AI assistants

Visit Chatbots to chat with Freida for fitness advice or Nardo for nutrition tips. Ask them anything!

5

Keep your info up to date

Changed your weight or goal? Use Update Profile from the avatar menu at any time — your nutrition targets will update automatically.
